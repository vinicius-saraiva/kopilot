Brand Story

The Copilot
That Keeps Score

For people who want to stay on top of their car—without becoming a car person.

The Problem

Most people aren't car enthusiasts. They don't know when maintenance is due, what that dashboard light means, or why their car makes that weird noise. Things slip. Cars break down. Repairs cost more than they should.

The Insight

Maintenance isn't the problem—forgetting is. People already track their runs, their sleep, their spending. They just need the same clarity for their car.

"Kopilot turns car maintenance from a forgotten chore into visible progress you can track and own."

The Name

Kopilot comes from rally racing, where the co-pilot navigates hazards and keeps the driver informed. The "K" makes it distinctive, modern, and globally recognizable. It's your car's co-pilot—always watching, always reminding, always ready.

Brand Values

Proactive

We tell you before things break. Reminders that actually matter, at the right time.

Clear

No jargon. No mystery codes. Just plain language and obvious next steps.

Motivating

Every oil change is progress. Every service is a streak. Make maintenance feel like winning.

Complete

Your car's full history in one place. Good for you now, great for resale later.

Brand Voice

Coach,
Not Critic.

Direct, motivating, and clear. We help you stay on track without making you feel bad.

Direct

Say what needs to happen. No hedging, no filler. "Oil change due in 800km" beats "You might want to consider scheduling an oil change soon."

Motivating

Frame maintenance as progress, not punishment. Celebrate streaks. Acknowledge effort. Make doing the right thing feel good.

Clear

No jargon. No codes without explanations. If we mention "P0420," we tell you what it means in plain language right after.

Kopilot Says

"Oil change in 800 km. You're on a 3-month streak—keep it going!"

Kopilot Doesn't Say

"WARNING: Your vehicle may be approaching the recommended oil change interval. Please consult your owner's manual."

Kopilot Says

"Front left tire is low: 24 PSI. Target is 32. Here's how to fix it."

Kopilot Doesn't Say

"Tire pressure monitoring system has detected a deviation from optimal parameters."
