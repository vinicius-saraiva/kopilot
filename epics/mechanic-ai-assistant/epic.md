## Summary

An AI assistant that helps non-car-people understand and triage car issues through text, voice, photo, and video interaction. It interprets symptoms, assesses severity, provides DIY guidance, and helps users communicate with mechanics.

---

## Key Capabilities (high-level)

- Symptom interpretation ("Squealing when braking = likely worn brake pads")
- Severity assessment ("Safe to drive" vs. "Stop immediately")
- DIY guidance for simple checks
- Mechanic referral with what to tell them
- Multimodal input: text, voice, photo, video
